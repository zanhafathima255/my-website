About VibeVault
Where fandom meets style.

VibeVault is a tiny corner of the internet where keychains, phone charms, stickers and tote patches are treated like love letters to your favorite characters, shows and artists.

🎨 Pop-culture obsessed designers 💌 Fan-first drops 🌈 Gen-Z aesthetic
Why we exist

Because fans deserve cute merch.

We were tired of boring, overpriced merch that didn’t match the way we actually dress, scroll, or spam our friends with screenshots. So we started making pieces we wished existed:

  • Affordable, iconic, aesthetic accessories that feel like part of your outfit.
  • Drops inspired by trending shows, anime arcs, comebacks & meme culture.
  • Designs that look good both IRL and in mirror selfies.

Tiny team energy

A tiny team of fans.

🎀
The VibeVault crew
Designers · Pack order nerds · Social meme posters

We’re a small creator-run studio that started by printing stickers on a bedroom desk and packing orders on the floor. Every charm, sticker and patch is something we genuinely wanted to wear on our own bags, phones and laptops.

Today, we still hand-check each batch, obsess over color accuracy and scream in the DMs when you tag us in your photos. That’s the entire business plan, tbh.

FAQs
How long does shipping take?
Orders typically ship in 2–4 business days. Delivery is usually within 4–8 days depending on your city.
Are stickers waterproof?
Yes! Our vinyl stickers are waterproof & fade-resistant. Perfect for bottles, laptops & cases.
Do you accept returns?
If your item arrives damaged or misprinted, email us within 7 days with photos and we’ll fix it.
Do you do creator / brand collabs?
Absolutely. Use the form and add "Collab" in your subject – we’ll get back with ideas & mockups.
